Printing encoders

Printing a real, physical encoder that you can use on your robot is the whole point of WheelEncoderGenerator. The application can print encoder patterns to scale using native print routines.

Once you've designed an encoder, print it out by selecting Print from the File menu or click the Print tool in the toolbar. A familiar print dialog will launch.

Printing tips

If the printed encoder is bigger or smaller than the diameter you specified, ensure the printer is configured to print at 1:1 (or 100%) scaling.

Adhesive or transparent encoders

Printing onto self-adhesive labels is one way to easily transfer your encoder onto a rigid disc without having to print the pattern directly onto the disc. Another option is to print the encoder onto transparency material and use a photo interrupter sensor rather than a reflective sensor.
