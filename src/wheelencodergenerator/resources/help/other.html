Other encoder settings

Additional encoder settings finalize the design for your particular application.

Inverting the encoder

Depending on whether the encoder sensor is active-low or active-high, the encoder disc pattern may need to be inverted. That is, the 1's represented by black instead of white. The inversion can be done in code as well, but inverting the encoder pattern is provided for convenience.

Invert the encoder by clicking the Invert encoder check box in the "Other" panel.
